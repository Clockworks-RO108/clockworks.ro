# Plant assistant

Plant Assistant is a concept invented from scratch by our team, which estabilishes the concept of learning about nature protection through practical activities, i.e. taking care of a plant with the help of a smart pot.

The concept was suggested toward implementation within the "Participatory Budgeting" challenge made by PROEDUS in partnership with the Bucharest City Hall, in the amount of 100,000 lei, successfully passing all stages and verifications, reaching the final stage.
